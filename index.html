About

Today digital design is taking over the world and it needs more designers with incredible skills. This course goes through the fundamentals of design and then deep dives into UX and UI. We go through user research, ideation, prototyping to visual design like color selection, typography and components of UI. This course aims at giving you an insight of what designers do on a daily basis and how they do it.
